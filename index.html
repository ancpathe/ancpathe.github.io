I stared down at the sheet I held. It had my name and the grades followed. Not what I had hoped for but something I thought I could exploit. My passion for a career was split and I was led into a dilemma. Do I try to pursue law or step into the medical field? I always knew medicine would be a challenge, a challenge I wanted to conquer. Thus started my journey towards choosing a competent university and acquiring admission

My search for a university was widespread. Ranging from Latvia and Belarus to Australia. The competitive nature of the medical field and the difficulty to attain admission to a medical school is common knowledge. Keeping this in mind I had to traverse carefully and cleverly. It was then that Mr. P. Saravanan who serves as a Director/CFO at ANC suggested Pathe Study Medicine as an option to prospect a good university to pursue my tertiary education. I wanted to practice medicine in Sri Lanka but was also aware of the vibe in Sri Lanka with regards to Private Medical Colleges. Having completed my London A/Levels, I understood that continuing my education in a foreign nation would be the more viable alternative.
